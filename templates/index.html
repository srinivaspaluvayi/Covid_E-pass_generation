Covid-19 ePass

E Pass During lockdown

People can use this ePass to move around the state as well as to travel interstate.This pass is like proof of you being negative and allows you to go out,although in cases of emergency.

Vehicle pass during lockdown

This will provide e-pass for your vehicles.With the help of this pass it will become easier to move around and even travel interstate.However,along with this pass,you also need identity proof like your aadhar card.

Curfew epass

Whenever there are emergency situations curfew pass will help you to travel without any difficulty.This pass has become a boon to many citizens.
